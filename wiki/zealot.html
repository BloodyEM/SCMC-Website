Spawning

The Zealot spawns on Shakuras in large numbers. Group sizes range from around 0-20, and come densely packed together.

Drops

The Zealot has a rare chance of dropping either energy or a psi blade.

Behavior

Like every mob, when a player is within range of their visual range they will immediately begin pursuing them. Since they travel just above sprinting speed, they will catch up to players quickly. Their damage is comparable to vanilla mobs on hardmode, and with their greater-than zombie pigmen levels of mob attacking, it makes them a very dangerous foe on the planet. Other than that, they have regular path-finding like normal mobs and wont fall off tall drops.
