Spawning

Larva spawns in Char on Zerg creep, or hatch from cocoons that grow on the creep. They spawn in groups of 3-6, with another or more close by. Clusters of larva are spread quite far from eachother.

Drops

The only thing Larva drops is Zerg carapace T1.

Behavior

Larva is a peaceful mob which sits around and occasionally moves at a slow pace. They can only walk on Zerg creep, and will start taking damage if they tread anywhere else. They will not retaliate when attacked, and instead will run around randomly much like vanilla animals.
