Spawning

Larva cocoons spawn similarly to larva itself. They only spawn on Zerg creep in groups of 3-6, with 1 or more groups nearby. These clusters are widespread throughout Char.

Drops

Larva cocoons do not drop anything.

Behavior

Larva cocoons sit around and do nothing. They have 200 health making them hard to kill, if you wanted to. After a while, larva will hatch from the cocoon, destroying it in the process.
