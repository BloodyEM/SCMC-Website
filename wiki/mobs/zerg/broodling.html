Spawning

Whenever zerg flesh is broken in Char, a broodling will come crawling out of it.

Drops

Broodlings drop nothing upon death, apart from experience. Breaking zerg flesh to spawn a broodling will yield organic tissue from the block.

Behavior

Broodlings move at fast, baby-zombie like speeds. Despite being tiny, they can do on average a heart of damage, and have more health than a player. They can be dangerous in groups if not properly equipped or prepared.
